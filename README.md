#### [MWR Digital Library](https://www.dodmwrlibraries.org/) 

  Requires Military One Source [account](https://www.dodmwrlibraries.org/aafes/login). 

   

  Details: 

  Select from the featured items or use the search app to access online books, films, music and more. All of the libraries are free for active duty service members, veterans in transition, and immediate family. 

   

  Using MWR Digital Library you gain free access too:

1. Biography and Genealogy
	- Ancestry
	- Fold3 (access to historical US military records)
	- Gale In Context: Biography

	- ArtistWorks for Libraries
	- EBSCO LearningExpress
	- Gale OneFile: Vocations and Careers
	- Great Courses (offers access to hundreds of courses taught by internationally recognized university faculty members)
	- Interplay Learning (complete workforce training program/ Career pathways supported include HVAC, plumbing, electrical, solar and more.)
	- Mango Languages (offers over 70 languages and provides the 6 critical skills for learning a new language)
	- O'Reilly (world class business and technology interactive trainings)
	- Pass Your Class (Pass Your Class CLEP and DANTES/DSST study guides)
	- Universal Class (provides online continuing education courses)
	- World Book Student (interactive encyclopedia that comes to life through this digital format)
3. eBooks and Audiobooks
	- EBSCO eBooks (variety of topics such as self-help, hobbies and cooking)
	- hoopla (Access the latest in movies, music, audiobooks, eBooks, comics, and TV shows)

4. Many, many more!
	- Children's Corner
